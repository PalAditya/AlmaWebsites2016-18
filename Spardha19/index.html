Eligibility & Rules

Examination Structure

Spardha consists of compulsory Physics, Chemistry and Aptititude. For Students from 6th standard to 10th Standard Maths and Biology is compulsory. For 11th and 12th standard students they can choose one subject from Mathematics or Biology.

Examination Scheddule

The registration for the exam ends on 15th of December. From then onwards depending on availability of a perfect schedule, Spardha is conducted on a national platform in the month of December. The exact dates will be notified later.

Syllabus and Difficulty

As Spardha is a All India Aptitude cum Talent Search examination, the difficulty level is kept high to get the selection of the best students for prizes. Although NCERT syllabus of that year is kept as the satndard syllabus

Registration

Registration of the examination can be done through the Regional Coordiantor and the exam cost is Rs. 100. After registration on the website, student receives a mail with his roll number. This will be used as a basis for further processes.

PRIZES

Spardha comes with an initiative of talent hunting and believes in rewarding the talent across our country. Top 3 candidates get an opportunity to visit IIT Bhubaneswar. A participation certificate is given to every participant. Total Prizes of worth Rs. 1,00,000 are being offered to the studets through Alma Fiesta.
