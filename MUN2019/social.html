Social

The former and the first IITBBSR MUN, at the end of each day's session, witnesses a very enthusiastic delegates at the evening events. All the delegates were given free entry, keeping in mind the duress of the day. The evenings' renowned and epic performances went a long way in venting out all the heat as the spectators mingled and enjoyed as the twilight merged into soft, caressing nights.

Here is a brief description of the events:

* The Inaugural Night was graced by the august presence of the Padma Shree awardee, world renowned sand artist Sudarsan Pattnaik who inspired the audience to follow their dreams and reach the pinnacle of success. The night became melodious with the performance of the award winning classical violinist Kala Ramnath, also known as 'The Singing Violin'. This was followed by an energetic Gotipua dance by the Gotipua Dance group from the state of Odisha.

* The evening of the second day saw a huge gathering of invigorated students swaying to the scintillating beats of DJ NYK and DJ Ansh. The throng seemed indefatigable as the skilled artist entertained them late into the night. An indelible experience.

* The concluding night featured a powerful performance of the prodigious Nikhil D'Souza who rocked the stage for hours and made the night an intoxicated one for the participants.
