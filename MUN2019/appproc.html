Application Procedure

1. Apply under the 'Applications' section of the website.

2. Upon completion of the form, you will receive an email confirming your application. If you don't get one, drop an email at info.mun@almafiesta.com

3. Date for Allotments are as follows:-
  a. First Round : 29 November, 2016
  b. Second Round : 14 December, 2016
  c. Third Round : 5 January, 2017

4. Upon getting an allotment, you will receive an email with the payment procedures.

5. Allotments will be confirmed once you pay the fees.

6. The payment window for the allotments are as follows:-
  a. Payment window for First Round is from 29 November, 2016 to 4 December, 2016
  b. Payment window for Second Round is from 14 December, 2016 to 18 December, 2016
  c. Payment window for Third Round is from 5 January, 2017 to 8 January, 2017

7. The payment methods are:-
  a. Through Credit card
  b. Through Debit card
  c. Through Net Banking

8. Upon completion of payment, you shall receive a confirmation by email.
