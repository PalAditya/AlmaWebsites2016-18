About Us

Aghaaz is the much awaited fashion show which provides a common stage to dashing personalities belonging to different colleges from all over India to show off their skills. For all the heartbreakers, this is your chance to capture the world's attention. Let us all let our attires speak this globally acknowledged language-the language of fashion, and simultaneously admire the beauty, grace and elegance exhibited by others which can pacify any beast.

A closer look

Heart of our college

Alma Fiesta is the annual socio-cultural fest of IIT Bhubaneswar, which sets the heart of every IITian pounding and allows them to lose themselves in a plethora of emotions and festivity. Aghaaz, the Fashion show is the harbinger to this mega-carnival, and an event you simply can't miss.

Keep your Eyes Open

Aghaaz exhibits, encourages, inculcates and inspires fashion, taking it to a new height with each and every passing year. This event will certainly dazzle you with its grandeur, and if you blink, you are sure to miss something amazing.

Distance no barrier

Aghaaz, in its past editions, has inspired participation from all over Odisha, making it an ideal platform for emerging talents to showcase their potential. Plane, train, bus or auto, whatever be the mode of transport, distance should never be a barrier between you and your dreams anyway

It's your turn now

Is the inner fashionista in you excited yet? Deep inside, do you feel the urge pushing you forth to show the world what you can do? If so, what are you waiting for? Clock's ticking, so hurry up and register to set the stage on fire on the designated day and be the part of an unforgettable experience

Rules

01

Age Limit:

Only contestants below 25 years are allowed.

02

Online Registration:

Participants will have to register online with valid information. Any wrong information will lead to cancellation of participation

03

Rounds:

There will be two rounds :
a) Round 1: Casual Wear.
b) Round 2: Fusion Cuisine

04

Apparels for Aghaaz:

For the second round, the participants can choose clothing from Aghaaz's fashion partner, Lifestyle's store in Esplanade One!

05

No complaints

The decision of the judges will be final & binding on all participants

06

Identification

Participants must bring two passport size photographs alongside them.

07

Fees

The participation cost is Rs. 500 per participant
Note: In case the participants do offline registration and choose to pay directly at the venue, they must pay an additional 100 Rs.
